Scookie Recipe

Ingredients -- 1 Batch

  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 1/2 cups of oats
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 bags of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 bag of butter scotch chips
  • 1 bag of heath bar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Soften butter
  3. Add all dry ingredients to bowl and mix thoroughly
  4. Mix in butter and eggs
  5. Add vanilla extract
  6. Form mixture into balls and place on a baking sheet
  7. Put baking sheet into the oven for 8 minutes. Do not let the cookies brown
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes before enjoying!
