Turkey Burger Recipe

Ingredients -- Makes 4

  • 1 lb ground turkey (85%-95% lean)(not fat free)
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonaisse
  • 1/2 cup of Panko seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Beat the egg white
  2. Add all ingredients into a bowl and mix thoroughly
  3. Form into individual patties
  4. Grill until 160 degrees internal
  5. Add any condiments or toppings
